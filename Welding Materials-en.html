Solder Paste

Dyfenco's lead-free solder paste complies with RoHS regulations and is designed for high-reliability electronic assembly. It offers excellent wetting, printing stability, and low voiding performance.

  • Alloy Composition: Sn96.5 / Ag3.0 / Cu0.5
  • Powder Size: T4 (20–38 μm)
  • Melting Point: 217°C
  • Flux Type: Halogen-free / No-clean

Applications: SMT process, PCB assembly, automotive electronics, servers, industrial equipment

Solder Wire

Dyfenco’s solder wire is widely used in hand soldering, electrical repairs, and specialty metal bonding processes. Available in halogen-free, no-clean, and various alloy-compatible formulas.

  • Halogen-Free Series: HF961 (excellent wetting), HF1072 (low spatter)
  • No-Clean High Performance: GamPlus1688, 2799, HDF-2730
  • Special Base Metal Soldering: Ni200 (nickel), AI-201 (aluminum), SS-302 (stainless steel)
  • Low-Temperature Models: LWT-137

Solder Bar

Dyfenco’s solder bars include high-silver, low-silver, silver-free, and MB high-temperature series. Suitable for wave soldering, manual filling, and large-area soldering.

  • High Silver: SAC305 (E9650), SAC305CX
  • Low Silver: SAC0307, 0307CX
  • Silver-Free: NAP100, NAP100CX
  • MB High-Temperature Series: MB-SAC305, MB-SAC0307, MB-NAP100, MB-D9930C (Patented Alloys)
