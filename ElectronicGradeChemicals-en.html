Liquid Flux

Dyfenco's liquid flux series is designed for wave soldering, no-clean processes, and eco-friendly applications. It offers excellent stability and reliability.

  • Wave Soldering: ZHF7600, K6
  • No-Clean Compatibility: K8418A, K8418B, K8600, K500, LF970
  • Water-Based VOC-Free: VOC228
  • Dipping Flux: TF15, WH2208
  • Spray Tin Application: WA2018

Tacky Flux

Dyfenco's tacky flux is ideal for BGA packaging, PCB assembly, and low-temperature/high-density component rework. It features high activity with low residue.

  • Halogen-Free High Reliability: QT459DE, DWQ401
  • No-Clean Lead-Free: PN436, PN436T
  • No-Clean Leaded: AM218FT
  • No-Clean Low Temp Alloy: XT963

Electronic Cleaners

Dyfenco's electronic cleaners are formulated as environmentally friendly alternatives to chlorofluorocarbons and other harmful substances. They are engineered for performance, stability, and predictability, applicable to various industrial cleaning needs.

  • Types: Solvent-based, Water-based
  • Application Methods: Wiping, Spraying, Immersion, In-line cleaning
  • Eco-Friendly Features: Non-hazardous, Biodegradable

Suitable for: PCB, steel plates, automotive parts, SMT stencil cleaning
