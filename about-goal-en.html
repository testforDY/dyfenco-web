GOAL

At DYFENCO, we are dedicated to advancing soldering material technologies to meet the fast-changing needs of the electronics industry. Through continuous improvement and strategic planning, we aim to deliver excellence and drive sustainable growth. Innovation is key to our competitiveness — we integrate R&D, marketing, and talent to stay ahead. At the same time, we uphold our environmental responsibility by promoting green processes and developing lead-free, halogen-free materials.

With integrity, we create shared value for our team and reduce collective risk.

Corporate Management: Integrity fosters shared value among employees and reduces organizational risk.
Customer Relations: Delivering high-quality products, reliable services, and competitive pricing is the foundation of lasting partnerships.
Employee Rights: We comply with labor laws and fundamental rights, ensuring a safe working environment and protecting employee interests.
Innovation & Value: We improve product quality, production efficiency, and process innovation to maximize customer value.
Social Responsibility: We actively engage in environmental protection and community involvement.

Driving Sustainability Forward

Dyfenco remain committed to the principles of integrity in management, sustainable innovation, and customer service, while actively promoting sustainability, corporate social responsibility, and positive impact.
Our sustainability efforts focus on five key areas: corporate governance, customer relations, employee rights, innovation, and environmental and social responsibility.
