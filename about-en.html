About Dyfenco

Dyfenco Co., Ltd. was established in 1979 by a team of experts with extensive experience in materials science and packaging processes. The company specializes in the research, development, and manufacturing of electronic soldering materials, continuously providing high-quality and reliable raw materials for the global electronics industry.

Our product range includes Solder Paste, Solder Wire, Solder Bar, Flux, Tin Anodes, Solder Preforms, and Electronic packaging adhesives. These products are widely applied in advanced manufacturing processes such as PCB assembly, automotive electronics, LED, semiconductor packaging, personal computers, and communication devices.
